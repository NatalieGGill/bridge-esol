For Families

School information should be understandable.

Families deserve clear explanations, respectful communication, and a real voice in their children's education. These steps can help you prepare for conversations with school staff.

  1. 1

    Write down your goal

    What do you want to understand or change?

  2. 2

    Gather examples

    Bring grades, messages, student work, or dates.

  3. 3

    Ask for language support

    Request interpretation or translated information when needed.

  4. 4

    Confirm the next step

    Before leaving, agree on who will do what and by when.
